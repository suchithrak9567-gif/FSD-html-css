Mountains are large landforms and structures of different shapes that have risen prominently above the surrounding terrain. These appear at a significant elevation with steep slopes and distant peaks or ridges. These provide the natural allure that is formed through erosion, volcanic eruptions, or tectonic activity showcasing unique geological features and factors. Apart from being a natural allure, these play a crucial role in hosting diverse ecosystems supporting unique wildlife, and influencing climatic patterns that foster biodiversity. They are also known to offer spirituality, being a cultural and recreational site in the history of humanity. This article provides you with information on how mountains are formed, types of mountains, differences between mountains and plateaus, and other fascinating facts that motivate you to expand your knowledge and explore them!
